Completed

Palettable | Streamlit, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

During the AstonHack 2025, my team developed Palettable. This tool worked to colour analyse and to organise user's wardrobes to increase assessability and usability within the Fashion industry. Our colour analysis program takes a live photograph via webcam or an uploaded image and picks out the colours. Based on these hues and the answers to a short quiz, your seasonal palette is calculated and colours are suggested. Our wardrobe feature enables users to upload their clothing items and browse through these. It works to help users remember which clothes they have and to easily put together outfits without having the big mess of physically sorting through your clothes.
